Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a research grant?

To apply for a research grant, visit our Research Grants page and follow the application process for the specific grant you're interested in.

What is the evaluation process for grant proposals?

Grant proposals undergo a rigorous evaluation process, including initial screening, peer review, and final assessment by our evaluation committee.

How often do I need to submit progress reports?

For most grants, progress reports are required on a quarterly basis. Specific requirements may vary depending on the grant program.
