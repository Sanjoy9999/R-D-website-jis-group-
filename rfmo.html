Research Funding Management Office (RFMO)

About RFMO

The Research Funding Management Office (RFMO) is responsible for managing seed funding, evaluating research proposals, and overseeing the progress of funded projects.

Seed Funding

Our seed funding program aims to support innovative research ideas in their early stages. Researchers can apply for seed funding to develop their concepts and gather preliminary data.

Apply for Seed Funding

Evaluation Process

  1. Submit your research proposal
  2. Initial screening by RFMO
  3. Peer review by subject matter experts
  4. Presentation to the evaluation committee
  5. Final decision and funding allocation

Quarterly Evaluation and Reporting

Funded projects are required to submit quarterly progress reports. These reports help us track the project's advancement and provide necessary support.
