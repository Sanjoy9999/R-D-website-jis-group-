Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Our platform supports a wide range of research fields, including but not limited to natural sciences, social sciences, engineering, and humanities. We provide tools and resources adaptable to various research methodologies and disciplines.

We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard encryption protocols and regularly perform security audits to ensure the highest level of protection for your research data. Additionally, we offer customizable access controls, allowing you to manage who can view or edit your research materials.

Our platform is designed to facilitate collaboration across institutions and even countries. You can easily invite collaborators, share resources, and work together in real-time, regardless of geographical boundaries.

We offer multiple levels of support depending on your plan. All users have access to our comprehensive documentation and community forums. Pro and Enterprise users benefit from priority email support, while Enterprise users also receive dedicated phone support and personalized onboarding.
